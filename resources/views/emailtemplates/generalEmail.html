What is the Preferred Freelancer Program?

 
 

The Preferred Freelancer Program is a competitive, highly curated group of elite skilled professionals. They have been through our extensive vetting and are trusted members of our community. They have access to high value projects from the world's leading enterprises such as IBM, Facebook, and NASA, just to name a few. Preferred Freelancers are our top performing freelancers on the platform. They receive curated invitations to projects from our global team of in-house Recruiters. 

 

Benefits of being a Preferred Freelancer

 

  • Exclusive curated invitations to high value Recruiter projects.
  • No upfront charges (only on Recruiter projects)
  • Tips and tricks for profile improvement
  • Premium customer support from the Preferred Freelancer Team
  • Preferred Freelancer badge to help you stand out from the crowd
  • All your bids will be sealed creating unique proposals for each client

Applying to become a Preferred Freelancer is free, but to maintain the integrity of the program, there are strict minimum requirements and other criteria in place that must be met. Our Preferred Freelancer Team will review your application and will contact you in no more than 5 business days.

 

Minimum Entry Requirements

  • Must be part of the top 3% of freelancers in at least one of your skills.
  • Your profile should include a well written biography, a professional profile photo and at least six items in your portfolio (Here are a few tips on how to build a profile)
  • You should be on a Membership Plan
  • Should not have active warnings or penalties on your profile
  • Being active on the platform in the past 90 days is necessary
  • Your KYC must be approved and your email verified

  • Your reviews should be great!

  • Not having more than three 1 star reviews in the last 365 days.
  • Pass the Preferred Freelancer Service Agreement exam with at least an 80% score
